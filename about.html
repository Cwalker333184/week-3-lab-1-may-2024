Popular Games

Mario was first introduced in 1981 in the arcade game Donkey Kong,
created by Shigeru Miyamoto and developed by Nintendo.
In this game, Mario was originally called" Jumpman and was a carpenter
trying to rescue Pauline from Donkey Kong.

Mario became the star of his own game in 1983 with Mario Bros., where
he and his brother Lugi were plumbers battling enemies in the sewers.
However, it was Super Mario Bros. (1985) on the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES)
that truly made Mario a household name. This game introduced
the Mushroom Kingdom, Princess Peach, and the battle against Bowser,
shaping the platforming genre and revolutionizing video games.
